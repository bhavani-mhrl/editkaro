Long-Form VIDEOS

Longform video editing is the process of editing extended video content (10 minutes to several hours). It’s used for films, documentaries, podcasts, webinars, and long YouTube tutorials. Unlike shortform editing, which is fast-paced and optimized for social media, longform editing focuses on storytelling, pacing, and depth. Get your long-form video professionally edited by our expert editors. Move from raw footage to finished content in 3-5 days per video.
